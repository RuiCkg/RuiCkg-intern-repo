## Research & Learn

### What are the main Agile ceremonies, and what is their purpose?

- _Daily Stand-ups:_
  - A 15-minute daily meeting for team members to sync, discuss progress, and identify blockers.
- _Sprint Planning (Scrum) vs. Continuous Prioritization (Kanban):_
  - The team defines the goal and selects items from the product backlog to complete.
  - Continuous prioritisation is deciding on priority of tasks and ensuring highest priority is done first
- _Retrospectives:_
  - Held at the end of a sprint, the team reflects on their process, tools, and relationships.
- _Backlog Refinement:_
  - An ongoing, often mid-sprint, meeting to review, size, and prioritize upcoming backlog items.

### How do Agile teams collaborate asynchronously and across time zones?

Teams can use tools like Jira and Slack for updates across different time zones.

## Reflection

### How do Agile ceremonies help with communication and alignment?

Ceremonies ensure team members understand each other's activities and maintain communication. They also identify members falling behind so others can provide support.

### Which ceremony do you think is most important for your role, and why?

Development is an ongoing process requiring the efforts of many people, making daily stand-ups crucial for developers. Since some features are closely interdependent with others, developers must maintain constant communication to ensure a smooth workflow during the sprint.
