## Research & Learn
### What are examples of professional vs. unprofessional behaviour in a casual work environment? (Hint: Think about communication, meeting etiquette, and teamwork.)
- **Communication:** clear, concise language vs unclear, fragmented messages
- **Meeting etiquette:** always being on time, well prepared, listening well when others speak vs being late, not prepared, not listening while others speak
- **Teamwork:** respect each members, be responsible for your tasks and deadlines vs not respect members, not be responsilbe for your tasks

### How can you give and receive feedback professionally? (e.g., focus on actions, not people; assume good intent; ask for clarification instead of reacting defensively)
When giving feedback, I aim to be respectful and constructive, using clear examples and assuming positive intent. When receiving it, I listen actively without getting defensive, ask clarifying questions when needed, and reflect objectively to take meaningful action.
