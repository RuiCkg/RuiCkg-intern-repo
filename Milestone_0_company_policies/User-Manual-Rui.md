# Creating Your "User Manual for Me" - Rui Chosa

## Research & Learn
### What is a “User Manual for Me”, and why do teams use them?
A User Manual for Me is a document to explain their preferred working styles, strengths, weaknesses, and communication preferences to colleagues. Teams use them to break down communication barriers, speed up team integration, and reduce misunderstandings.

### What categories are helpful to include? (e.g., communication style, work habits, feedback preferences)
- My role
- How I work Best
- Communication Style
- Collaboration & Teamwork
- Focus & Productivity
- Feedback & Growth
- Fun Facts & Quirks

### How can writing a personal user manual improve teamwork and reduce misunderstandings?
Writing a personal user manual reduce misunderstanding by clarifying expectations and sharing how best to collaborate with a specific co-worker

- Sunday 8:00 - 11:00

## How I Work Best  
- I do my best work when: 
    - I am alone in a quiet space
    - I plan out my deadlines
    - Understand the task at hand
- I struggle when:
    - My schedule is busy
    - The task is difficult

## Communication Style  
- I prefer messages to be short and not too formal. 
- The best way to reach me is:
    Discord and Email
- If you need something urgent, do this:
    Send a message and email me so I can tell it's urgent

## Collaboration & Teamwork  
- I work best in a team when:
    - Everyone is genuinely committed and takes ownership of their responsibilities.
    - Team members respect each other’s ideas and create space for open discussion.
    - Everyone frequently messages and it is easy to tell that someone is participating
- My biggest pet peeve in teamwork is:
    - When someone stops responding or avoids communication without explanation.
    - When a team member is physically present but doesn’t actively engage in discussions or tasks.
- If I seem quiet in a meeting, it usually means:
    - I’m carefully listening and looking for the right moment to share my thoughts.

## Focus & Productivity  
- I stay focused by:
    - Listening to music
    - Working in a quiet space
- Things that distract me:
    - Loud environments
- The best way to support my productivity is:
    - letting me be in a quiet environment

## Feedback & Growth  
- I like feedback to be:
    - Direct and to the point
    - Honest and reasonable
- When I make a mistake, the best way to let me know is;
    - Telling me ASAP
    - Will appriciate if you give me any suggestions what to do
- I appreciate it when teammates:
    - Give me feedback
    - Help me when I'm stuck
