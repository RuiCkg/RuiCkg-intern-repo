# Understanding of Focus Bear's mission and vision - Rui Chosa

## Why do you think Focus Bear was created?

Focus Bear was created because the founder, Jeremy, found that other productivity apps were not working well for him. Instead of relying on multiple different apps, he wanted to build one app that combined the best features of competing productivity tools into a single platform that actually worked for people like him.

## What problem is Focus Bear solving?

Focus Bear is solving the problem of distraction and lack of structure, especially for people with ADHD or autism. It helps users stay focused on tasks and build healthy habits through features like focus mode, morning routine checklists, and break reminders. It reduces distractions and creates structure in daily life.

## Why do you think this mission is important?

I think this mission is important because more people are being diagnosed with ADHD and autism, even later in life. Many traditional productivity systems are not designed with neurodivergent people in mind. There is a growing need for tools that are inclusive and supportive. Focus Bear addresses that gap by designing features that help people manage focus and routines in a practical way.

## How does Focus Bear’s work align with your personal values or interests?

I think this kind of app is useful not only for people with ADHD or autism, but for anyone who struggles with distractions. I value inclusivity and creating tools that help people perform at their best. Even though I do not personally know someone with these challenges, I respect the mission of supporting a group that is often misunderstood.

## Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?

Even though I have not been diagnosed with ADHD or autism, I sometimes struggle with distractions. For example, when working on assignments without immediate deadlines, I often get distracted by my phone. I think Focus Bear’s focus mode could help reduce those distractions and keep me accountable, helping me stay on track and complete tasks more efficiently.
