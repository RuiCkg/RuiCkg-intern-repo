# What Do You Want to Learn & Contribute? - Rui Chosa

## What do you hope to learn from this internship?
During this internship, I hope to learn how developers communicate and collaborate in a real industry environment, and how projects are managed from planning to delivery.

## What skills are you most excited to develop?
I'm excited to develop modern technology stacks and agile workflows, especially in frontend development. I’m really keen to learn from professional developers at Focus Bear and understand their approach to building clean, responsive, and user-friendly interfaces.

## What do you think you can contribute to the team?
I hope to contribute to real features, improve both my coding and communication skills, and receive feedback that helps me grow.

## What is one area you feel less confident in that you’d like to improve?
I feel less confident in understanding large codebases quickly, so I want to improve my ability to read and debug existing code.

## What does success in this internship look like for you?
It looks like completing tasks with good quality, getting positive feedback from supervisor, attending meetings and feeling more confident working in a team environment.
